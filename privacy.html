Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-06-30

Soul Exercise — Daily Koans (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

We do not require account registration, and we do not collect personal profile data such as name, phone number, or address.

Practice records (such as answered koans and reflection trends) are stored locally on your device for app functionality.

In-app purchase processing is handled by Apple through StoreKit. We do not directly receive your payment card information.

2. How We Use Data

  • To unlock purchased content and restore purchases.
  • To provide in-app features such as Daily Koan, Insight Mirror, and Zen Emergency usage limits.
  • To maintain app stability and basic operation.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising.

4. Data Retention

Local practice data remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear device data.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age required by your local laws. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
